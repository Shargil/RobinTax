# Crypto / Digital Asset Income Confirmation

**What it is**: Capital gains from crypto / NFTs — not included in regular Form 867 (טופס 867).

## Where to find it

- **Transaction reports** from the relevant exchanges (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, Israeli exchanges, etc.) — usually under "tax documents" or via API export.
- **Wallet transaction history** for on-chain trades.
