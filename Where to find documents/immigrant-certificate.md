# Immigrant Certificate (תעודת עולה)

**What it is**: Grants 3 credit points (נקודות זיכוי) in the first 18 months, 2 in the next 12, 1 in the following 12. Also relevant for exemptions on foreign income and the temporary provision (הוראת שעה) starting 5/11/2025.

## Where to find it

- **Ministry of Aliyah and Integration (משרד העלייה והקליטה)**: Certificate is given upon arrival in Israel.
- Replacements / copies — request via the Ministry of Aliyah and Integration online services.
