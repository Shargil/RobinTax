# Form 116א (טופס 116א) + Medical Confirmations — Incapacitated Relative / Child with a Disability

**What it is**: Request for tax credit for a relative with a disability. 2 credit points (נקודות זיכוי) per qualifying child/relative, including a child with a severe learning disability (לקות למידה חמורה) / ADHD diagnosed by a placement committee (ועדת השמה).

## Where to find it

- **Form 116א (טופס 116א)**: Israel Tax Authority site — [gov.il/he/service/itc-request-for-tax-credits-disabled-relative](https://www.gov.il/he/service/itc-request-for-tax-credits-disabled-relative).
- **Form 127 (טופס 127)** — Medical certificate signed by a specialist doctor.
- **Placement committee (ועדת השמה) / Ministry of Education diagnosis committee** — confirmations.
