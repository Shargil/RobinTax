# Rental Income Confirmation (הכנסה מהשכרת דירה)

**What it is**: Income from rent. Up to the monthly exemption ceiling (5,654 ₪ in 2024, updated annually) — exempt. Above that — taxable (10% in the short track or marginal rate).

## Where to find it

- **Rental contract** (חוזה שכירות) — signed copy.
- **Receipts** or confirmation of deposits into the bank account (bank statements showing rent transfers).
