# Rental / Apartment Contract — for Settlement Residency Proof

**What it is**: Supporting evidence of residency in a beneficial settlement (יישוב מזכה) for Section 11 (סעיף 11) eligibility.

## Where to find it

- **Original signed rental contract (חוזה שכירות)** for each relevant tax year.
- Supplement to the residency confirmation from the local authority.
