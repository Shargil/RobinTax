# Returning Resident / Veteran Returning Resident Certificate (תושב חוזר / תושב חוזר ותיק)

**What it is**: Exemption on foreign income — 10 years for a veteran returning resident (תושב חוזר ותיק) after 10+ years abroad; 5 years for a regular returning resident after 6+ years abroad.

## Where to find it

- **Ministry of Aliyah and Integration (משרד העלייה והקליטה)** — returning resident certificate.
- **Israel Tax Authority (רשות המסים)** — confirmation for tax purposes.
