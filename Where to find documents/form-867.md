# Form 867 (טופס 867) — Capital Gains Tax Withholding on Tradable Securities

**What it is**: Annual summary of capital gains/losses (רווחי/הפסדי הון), interest, dividends, and management fees — from banks and investment houses.

## Where to find it

- **Customer area** at every bank or investment house (Discount, Mizrahi, IBI, Meitav Dash, Altshuler Shaham, etc.).
- Downloadable online — usually under a "tax documents" / "מסמכי מס" section.
- For a multi-broker investor: must collect Form 867 (טופס 867) from each separately.
