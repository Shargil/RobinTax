# Form 119 (טופס 119) + Eligibility Certificate for Degree / Professional Certificate

**What it is**: 0.5–1 credit points (נקודות זיכוי) per year after completing studies, for the same number of years as the length of studies (up to 3 years).

## Where to find it

- **Form 119 (טופס 119)**: Israel Tax Authority site — [gov.il/he/service/itc119](https://www.gov.il/he/service/itc119).
- **Eligibility certificate (אישור זכאות)**: From the academic institution / professional training body.
- **ID card (תעודת זהות)**: Required as supporting document.
