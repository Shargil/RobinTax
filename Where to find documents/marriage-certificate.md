# Marriage Certificate / Common-Law Partner Confirmation (תעודת נישואין / ידועים בציבור)

**What it is**: Relevant for unified/separate calculation (חישוב מאוחד/נפרד) and credit point for a non-working spouse.

## Where to find it

- **Rabbinate (רבנות)** / religious court / **Ministry of Interior** — marriage certificate.
- **Common-law partner (ידועים בציבור)**: Affidavit + supporting documents (joint bank account, joint lease, etc.).
