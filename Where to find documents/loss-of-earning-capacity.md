# Loss of Earning Capacity Insurance Confirmation (ביטוח אובדן כושר עבודה)

**What it is**: Recognized expense (הוצאה מוכרת) for a self-employed person; for a salaried employee (שכיר) usually included in the pension insurance — already in Form 106 (טופס 106). Relevant if purchased privately outside of employer-provided pension.

## Where to find it

- **Annual confirmation** from the insurance company.
