# Independent Deposit to Continuing Education Fund (קרן השתלמות)

**What it is**: For a self-employed person — deduction up to 4.5% of income. For a salaried employee (שכיר) — usually managed by the employer (already in Form 106 / טופס 106). Relevant if self-deposited.

## Where to find it

- **Annual confirmation** from the fund (קרן השתלמות) managing entity.
- Available in the customer area on the fund's site.
