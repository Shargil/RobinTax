# Pension / Old Age / Survivors Allowance Confirmation

**What it is**: Form 106 (טופס 106) from the payer of any allowance: insurance company, pension fund, or employer-payer.

## Where to find it

- **From the paying entity**: Insurance company, pension fund, or the National Insurance Institute (ביטוח לאומי) for old-age pension.
- Usually downloadable from the customer area on the payer's site.
