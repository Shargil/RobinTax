# Form 161 / 161א (טופס 161 / 161א) — Notice of Retirement from Work

**What it is**: Report of severance pay (פיצויי פיטורים), grants, redemption of vacation and sick days. Option to spread tax (פריסה) over up to 6 years.

## Where to find it

- **Form 161 (טופס 161)**: From the employer at the time of leaving work.
- **Form 161א (טופס 161א)**: Filled out with the tax assessor (פקיד שומה). Israel Tax Authority site: [gov.il/he/service/notice-of-retirement](https://www.gov.il/he/service/notice-of-retirement).
