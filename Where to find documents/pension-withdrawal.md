# Pension / Provident Fund Withdrawal Confirmation

**What it is**: Withdrawal at retirement age / death / disability from a provident fund (קופת גמל) / pension fund (קרן פנסיה) / continuing education fund (קרן השתלמות). Excess tax may have been withheld.

## Where to find it

- **Allowance / withdrawal confirmation** from the managing entity (insurance company / pension fund / provident fund manager).
