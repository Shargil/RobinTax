# Divorce Agreement / Alimony Court Ruling (פסק דין למזונות)

**What it is**: Grants credit points (נקודות זיכוי) to a single-parent head and the alimony payer.

## Where to find it

- **Family Affairs Court (בית המשפט לענייני משפחה)**: Court ruling.
- **Form 4440 (טופס 4440)**: Affidavit declaring custodial / alimony-paying parent status.
- **Bank**: Transfer confirmations showing alimony payments.
