# Self-Deposit Confirmation — Provident Fund (קופת גמל) / Pension Fund (קרן פנסיה) / Managers Insurance (ביטוח מנהלים)

**What it is**: Credit under Section 45א (סעיף 45א) — 35% on deposits up to ceiling; deduction under Section 47 (סעיף 47) — up to 11% of taxable income. For self-deposits beyond what's withheld through salary.

## Where to find it

- **Annual confirmation** from the pension fund / insurance company / provident fund.
- Available in the customer area on the managing entity's site.
