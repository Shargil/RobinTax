# Exemption Confirmation from the Tax Assessor (אישור פטור מפקיד שומה)

**What it is**: The final document recognizing the income tax exemption (after the medical committee).

## Where to find it

- **Tax assessor (פקיד שומה)**: After approval of the medical committee, the tax assessor's office issues the exemption confirmation.
- Held by the taxpayer; presented to employers and to Israel Tax Authority (רשות המסים) on file.
