# Confirmation of Tax Paid Abroad — Foreign Tax Credit (זיכוי כפל מס)

**What it is**: Credit on tax paid in a foreign country on income that is also taxable in Israel (under tax treaties).

## Where to find it

- **Withholding confirmation from the foreign entity** — foreign bank, foreign employer, foreign tax authority.
- Notarized translation may be required for documents not in Hebrew or English.
