# Form 169א (טופס 169א) + Medical Committee Confirmation — Section 9(5) Exemption (סעיף 9(5))

**What it is**: Full / partial income tax exemption for a medically disabled person (90%+, or 89%+ with specific weighting), or the blind.

## Where to find it

- **Form 169א (טופס 169א)**: Israel Tax Authority site — [gov.il/he/service/itc169a](https://www.gov.il/he/service/itc169a).
- **Submission to a medical committee (ועדה רפואית)** of National Insurance (ביטוח לאומי).
- **Supporting documents**: Medical protocols, full medical file, specialist reports.
- After approval — the committee issues the confirmation of medical disability percentage.
