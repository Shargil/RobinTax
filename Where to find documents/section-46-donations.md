# Donation Receipts Under Section 46 (סעיף 46)

**What it is**: 35% tax credit on cumulative donations to a recognized institution above the annual threshold (190 ₪ from 2019), up to a ceiling (30% of taxable income or ~10.5M ₪, whichever is lower).

## Where to find it

- **Original receipt from the institution** — must explicitly state "Recognized Institution Under Section 46" (מוסד מוכר לפי סעיף 46) and include the donor's ID number.
- Verify the institution: [Israel Tax Authority Section 46 eligibility simulator](https://www.gov.il/he/service/confirmation-of-donations).
- **2026 reform**: From 1/1/2026 — only a digital receipt reported to the Israel Tax Authority (רשות המסים) system grants the donor a credit. Manual / internal-system receipts will no longer be recognized.
