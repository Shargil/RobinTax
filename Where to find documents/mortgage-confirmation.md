# Mortgage Confirmation (אישור משכנתא)

**What it is**: Real interest on a mortgage may be recognized as a deduction when selling an asset (capital gains tax / מס שבח). Less relevant for a pure salaried employee unless an apartment was sold.

## Where to find it

- **Annual confirmation from the mortgage bank** — interest payments breakdown.
- Available in online banking under tax documents / annual confirmations.
