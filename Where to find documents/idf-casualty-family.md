# Confirmation for IDF Casualty Family / Bereaved Parent / Widow

**What it is**: Special tax entitlements for relatives of IDF casualties (יקיר, אלמן/אלמנת מערכה, הורה שכול).

## Where to find it

- **Families and Commemoration Department (אגף משפחות והנצחה)** at the Ministry of Defense — issues the relevant entitlement confirmation.
