# ID Card (תעודת זהות) + Appendix (ספח)

**What it is**: Used to identify the filer and details of family members (children, spouse).

## Where to obtain

- **Population Authority (רשות האוכלוסין) / Ministry of Interior (משרד הפנים)** — physical card and appendix.
- **Digital ID app** — official "Israeli digital ID" app issued by the Population Authority.
- Updates to the appendix (e.g., new child, marriage, divorce) should be done at the Population Authority before filing.
