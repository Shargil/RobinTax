# Form 4435 (טופס 4435) — Registered Spouse Declaration

**What it is**: Determines which spouse is the "registered spouse" (בן זוג רשום) for tax purposes; affects unified vs. separate calculation (חישוב מאוחד/נפרד).

## Where to obtain

- **Israel Tax Authority site (online)**: [gov.il/he/service/itc4435](https://www.gov.il/he/service/itc4435) — can be filled out and submitted electronically.
- **Submission deadline**: must be submitted at least 3 months before the start of the tax year.
- **Condition**: the income of the chosen "registered spouse" must be at least 25% of the other spouse's income in the year preceding the year of the declaration.
