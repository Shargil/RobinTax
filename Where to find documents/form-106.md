# Form 106 (טופס 106) — Salary and Tax Withholding Confirmation

**What it is**: Annual summary of salary, social contributions, tax withheld at source (ניכוי מס במקור), and credit points (נקודות זיכוי). The basis of every tax refund calculation.

## Where to obtain

- **From the employer**: usually issued by **31/3** of the following year. Must be received from **every** employer the person worked for during the tax year.
- **Personal area on the Israel Tax Authority site (רשות המסים)**: Form 106 is published in the personal area / through **שע"מ** (the Tax Authority's online system) once the employer reports it.
- **For an inactive / former employer who won't respond**: contact the Ministry of Labor (משרד העבודה) or go through the labor court (בית הדין לעבודה) if necessary.
- **Multiple employers**: a separate Form 106 from each one is required.
