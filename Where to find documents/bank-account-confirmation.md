# Bank Account Confirmation (אישור ניהול חשבון) / Voided Check

**What it is**: Verification of the bank account details for the refund transfer.

## Where to obtain

- **Bank app / online banking**: "אישור ניהול חשבון" (account management confirmation) screen — usually downloadable as PDF.
- **Bank branch**: request from the teller.
- **Voided check**: photo / scan of a check from the same account with "מבוטל" (voided) written across it.
- The document must clearly show: account holder name, bank, branch number, account number.
