# Tax Refund for Salaried Employees (החזר מס לשכירים) — Form 135

Covers the refund flow for a salaried Israeli employee who is **not** required to file a full annual return. Files **טופס 135** with רשות המסים to reclaim over-withheld PAYE. Does NOT cover self-employed — see [tax-refund-self-employed.md](tax-refund-self-employed.md). Does NOT cover refund timing/interest — see [tax-refund-timing.md](tax-refund-timing.md).

## When this track applies

- Income is wage / pension / annuity, monthly PAYE was deducted by the employer.
- Total annual income is below the mandatory-filing ceiling (~698,280 ₪ for 2023 tax year; check ITA for current year before relying on it).
- No business income, no significant capital income that triggers mandatory filing.
- Refund-eligible because PAYE during the year exceeded the real annual liability.

## The form: טופס 135

- "Short annual return for refund-requesting individuals."
- Filed **online** at gov.il (service `itc135`). Fully digital since tax year 2019 — including document upload.
- One form per tax year; refund requests are not bundled across years.

## Lookback window

- **Up to 6 tax years back.** In 2026 you can still file for tax years **2020–2025**.
- Deadline for the oldest open year is `31.12` of the sixth year (e.g., 2020 → 31.12.2026).
- After the lookback expires the right to the refund is lost.

## Required documents

> Comprehensive catalog (with the identification question and where to obtain each) lives in [tax-refund-documents-salaried.md](tax-refund-documents-salaried.md). The list below is the high-level baseline.

- תעודת זהות (ID copy).
- **טופס 106 from every employer**, every year claimed. Employers must issue 106 by end of March for the prior year. This is the single most critical document — without it the request will not be processed.
- אישור ניהול חשבון בנק — bank-account confirmation; refund is wired here.
- Backing evidence for each credit/deduction claimed:
  - Pension / קופת גמל / קרן השתלמות deposit statements
  - Donation receipts to recognized institutions (סעיף 46)
  - Periphery-town residency proof (יישוב מזכה)
  - Marital-status / single-parent / divorce documents
  - New-immigrant (עולה חדש) certificate
  - Disability certificates
  - Discharge papers (ended army / national service in the tax year)

## Common refund triggers

The recurring reasons monthly withholding overshot the real liability:

- **Partial-year employment** — worked only part of the year, or had unemployment gaps. PAYE assumes constant monthly income across 12 months.
- **Job change mid-year** — new employer may not have applied the right credit-point and bracket carryover.
- **Multiple employers without תיאום מס** — the secondary employer withheld at top marginal rate.
- **Unclaimed נקודות זיכוי** (credit points): new baby not reported on time, single parent, divorced/separated, academic degree, ended army service, עולה חדש, child with disability.
- **Independent pension / קרן השתלמות / קופת גמל deposits** beyond employer-reported amount. Salaried gets a **35% credit** on up to ~8,148 ₪/yr in 2026, capped at 7% of salary.
- **Donations** (סעיף 46) — not reported to employer during the year.
- **Periphery-town residency credit** (יישוב מזכה).
- **Severance pay** (פיצויי פיטורין) — taxed at source when an exemption applied.

## End-to-end flow

1. **Collect** all 106s and supporting documents for every year being claimed (one folder per year).
2. **Simulate** in an online refund simulator. If the simulation shows a *debt*, do **not** file blindly — see Gotchas.
3. **Identify on gov.il** (digital certificate, ID + bank, or ITA video identification).
4. **Open form 135** for the chosen tax year.
5. **Fill** all relevant sections, matching the wage figures from 106 exactly.
6. **Attach evidence** for every claimed credit/deduction.
7. **Submit** and track status in the personal area on gov.il.

## Gotchas

- **Filing can produce a debt, not a refund.** If PAYE was under-withheld (common with side income), 135 surfaces it. Run a simulator first.
- **Spousal calculation** — married couples generally file jointly. A separate calculation (חישוב נפרד) can yield a larger combined refund when both have independent income; conditions in the ordinance apply.
- **One year at a time** — a single 135 covers exactly one tax year.
- **Missing 106 = stalled refund.** Most refund rejections are missing documents, not bad math.
- **The lookback clock matters** — the year drops off at year-end six years out; don't queue a 2020 claim for January 2027.
